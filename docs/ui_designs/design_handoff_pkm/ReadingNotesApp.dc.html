The Beginning of Infinity

David Deutsch· ★★★★☆· Finished
#reading #epistemology #physics #explanation
The growth of knowledge consists of correcting misconceptions in our theories. — Ch. 1, The Reach of Explanations

## Core thesis

Good explanations — the ones that are hard to vary while still accounting for what they claim — are the real engine of progress. Deutsch argues that knowledge growth is unbounded: every advance links outward to Explanatory knowledge and reaches far beyond the problem that prompted it.

## Highlights

  • Knowledge is information with causal power — it tends to keep itself instantiated.
  • Problems are inevitable, but problems are soluble.
  • Optimism is the theory that all failures are caused by insufficient knowledge.
